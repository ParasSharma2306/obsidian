How to Use ChatLume

  1. Navigate to the Viewer tool.
  2. Enter your exact Display Name as it appears in the chat.
  3. Click "Browse" or Drag & Drop your exported WhatsApp .txt or .zip file.
  4. Wait a moment for the local parsing engine to organize your messages.
  5. Scroll, search, and analyze! Use the top-left menu icons to access your Profile, Analytics, and App Settings.
