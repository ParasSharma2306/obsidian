Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

The short version: Your chat files are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. If you create an account, we store only your email and subscription status — nothing from your chats.

1. Chat Data

ChatLume processes all chat files (WhatsApp .txt/.zip and Instagram .zip) entirely within your browser using the HTML5 File API and JavaScript. No chat content — messages, contact names, media, or metadata — is ever transmitted to any server operated by ChatLume or any third party.

You can disconnect from the internet after loading the viewer page and the tool will continue to work perfectly.

2. Account Data

If you choose to create a ChatLume account, we store:

  • Your email address
  • A hashed (bcrypt) password — we never store your plaintext password
  • Your subscription status (Free or Pro) and billing period dates
  • Account creation timestamp

This data is stored in a MongoDB database hosted on a private server. It is used solely to authenticate you and determine your subscription tier.

3. Payment Data

Payments are processed by Dodo Payments. ChatLume never receives or stores your card details. When you upgrade to Pro, Dodo Payments securely handles your payment information and notifies us of the subscription status via webhook. See Dodo Payments' Privacy Policy for details.

4. Analytics

ChatLume uses Umami, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics tool, to understand aggregate usage (page views, device types). Umami does not track individuals, store personal data, or use cookies. No IP addresses or fingerprints are recorded.

5. Cookies

If you sign in, we set a single httpOnly session cookie containing a signed JWT. This cookie is used only to authenticate your session — it contains your user ID and email. It expires after 7 days. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Dodo Payments — subscription billing
  • Umami Analytics — privacy-first page analytics
  • MongoDB Atlas — account database hosting

7. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, email hello@parassharma.com with the subject "Delete my account". We will permanently remove your data within 7 days.

8. Children

ChatLume is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of ChatLume after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions? Email hello@parassharma.com.
