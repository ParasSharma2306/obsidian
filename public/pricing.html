Frequently asked questions

Is my chat data safe?

Yes. Your files are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. The only things our server stores are your account email and subscription status.

What counts as "full chat history"?

Free users see the last 500 messages from their chat file. Pro users get access to the entire chat — no limit, no matter how long the conversation.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account page anytime. No questions asked. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cards and UPI via Dodo Payments — secure and instant. Dodo handles all payment processing; we never see your card details.
