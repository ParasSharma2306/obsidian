How to Export WhatsApp Chats

On iPhone (iOS)

  • Open the chat you want to export.
  • Tap the contact's name at the top.
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap Export Chat.
  • Choose "Without Media" (Recommended for speed) or "Attach Media".
  • Save the generated ZIP file to your Files app.

On Android

  • Open the chat. Tap the three dots (More options) in the top right.
  • Tap More > Export chat.
  • Choose "Without media" or "Include media".
  • Save the file to Google Drive or your local storage.
