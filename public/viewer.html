About ChatLume

ChatLume is a privacy-first WhatsApp chat viewer and analytics tool. It turns exported chats into a familiar interface with deep analytics while keeping processing fully inside your browser.

Capabilities

  • Zero Data Transfer: No uploads, no servers, completely private.
  • Client-side Parsing: Reads WhatsApp exports locally without storing chat data.
  • Memory-aware Media: Keeps ZIP attachments local and loads media only when it is needed.
  • Sliding Message Window: Adds nearby messages as you scroll and drops older rendered content from the opposite side.
  • ChatLume Wrapped: Extract emojis and hourly patterns to generate a personalized, shareable graphic.
  • Analytics: View total counts, media usage, and per-sender conversation shares.

Notes

  • ZIP exports provide the richest experience, and attachments are decoded lazily as they enter view.
  • Off-screen media object URLs are released automatically to keep memory usage low during long sessions.
  • Browser limits still apply. Very large media files can take a moment to decode, but the app avoids loading the entire export into the DOM.
