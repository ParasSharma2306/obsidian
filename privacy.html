Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

🔒 Your files never leave your device. ChatLume runs 100% in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any data.

How ChatLume works

ChatLume is a fully static website. When you upload a chat export file, it is read and processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. The file is never sent to any server. No data is stored beyond your current browser session.

What data we collect

None. ChatLume does not have a backend server, database, or analytics that track individual users. We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personal information. We do not read, store, or transmit your chat files.

Cookies and local storage

ChatLume uses sessionStorage only to remember whether you've dismissed the donation modal in the current browser session. This data is cleared automatically when you close the browser tab. We set no persistent cookies and use no tracking technologies.

Third-party services

ChatLume uses JSZip (loaded from Cloudflare CDN) on the WhatsApp Viewer page to extract zip files in-browser. This is the only third-party resource loaded, and it does not receive your chat data.

Your chat data

Your chat files are processed locally in your browser's memory. They are not stored on disk, indexed, or transmitted anywhere. When you close the page or reload it, the data is gone.

Children's privacy

ChatLume does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
